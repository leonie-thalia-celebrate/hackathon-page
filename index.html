The Challenge

Build What You Actually Need

Whether you're shipping your first single agent or designing systems where multiple specialized agents collaborate - bring your use case and build it. No pressure on the approach, just a focus on making things work.

Format
  • 🔨80–90% building time - bring your use case, work on it
  • 🤝Cross-company collaboration sessions - direct exchange between teams to find shared problems, spark synergies, and build on each other's work
  • 🖥️Daily Show & Tell - live demos only, no slide decks
  • 💡Knowledge rounds - share what worked, what broke
